Presentations to senior management can be quite tricky, so here are a few more tips (1/2)
Start with the conclusion or recommendation
Senior management are busy people, and they may not always bother reading through the entire deck to get to your main point. Put an executive summary upfront so they can get to the results and recommendations first before going through the other details.
